How Commissions Work

  1. Consultation: We discuss your idea, requirements, size, medium, and reference images.
  2. Quotation & Agreement: Based on the complexity, a quote is provided. Upon agreement, a deposit may be required.
  3. Sketching Phase: I begin the artwork, providing updates or initial drafts for your feedback.
  4. Revisions: Minor revisions are incorporated based on your input (scope for major changes might be limited after a certain stage).
  5. Completion & Delivery: Once finalized and approved, the remaining payment is made. The finished physical artwork is prepared for delivery or pickup (if local).

Digital copies can also be provided upon request.

About NSL Forge

NSL Forge is the creative outlet of Naman Lumbhani, an artist based in Navsari, Gujarat, specializing in traditional sketch artwork. With a passion for capturing detail and emotion through pencil and paper, Naman creates unique pieces ranging from realistic portraits to imaginative conceptual designs.

Each sketch is crafted with dedication, aiming to resonate with the viewer and the subject. Whether it's a cherished memory captured in a portrait or a brand new character brought to life, NSL Forge is where visions are meticulously forged into tangible art.
