How Art Requests Work: Your Artistic Journey

1

Initial Discussion

We discuss your vision, requirements (size, medium), and reference images to ensure I fully understand your idea.

2

Confirmation & Start

Upon confirmation of the project details, your art request is secured.

3

Sketching & Feedback

The creation begins! I'll provide initial drafts for your review and incorporate your valuable feedback.

4

Refinement & Approval

Minor revisions are made. Once you're delighted with the progress, we move towards finalization.

5

Finalization & Delivery

Your exquisite artwork is then carefully prepared for delivery or local pickup.

Digital high-resolution copies of your artwork can be provided upon request.

About NSL Forge

NSL Forge is the creative outlet of Naman Lumbhani, an artist based in Navsari, Gujarat, specializing in traditional sketch artwork. With a passion for capturing detail and emotion through pencil and paper, Naman creates unique pieces ranging from realistic portraits to imaginative conceptual designs.

Each sketch is crafted with dedication, aiming to resonate with the viewer and the subject. Whether it's a cherished memory captured in a portrait or a brand new character brought to life, NSL Forge is where visions are meticulously forged into tangible art.
