Conceptual Sketches

Visualize your ideas, concepts, or story moments through evocative and imaginative sketches.

Commission Process:

  1. Inquiry: Contact me with your idea, reference photos, desired style, and size.
  2. Discussion & Quote: We'll discuss the details, and I'll provide you with a quote and estimated timeline.
  3. Deposit: A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your commission slot.
  4. Creation: I'll get to work on your sketch! I can provide work-in-progress updates if requested.
  5. Review & Final Payment: Once the sketch is complete, I'll send you a watermarked preview for approval. After approval, the final 50% payment is due.
  6. Delivery: You'll receive the high-resolution digital file of your sketch (or physical artwork if arranged).

What's Included:

  • High-resolution digital file (e.g., JPG, PNG, PDF).
  • One round of minor revisions during the sketching phase.
  • Personal usage rights (commercial rights can be discussed).
